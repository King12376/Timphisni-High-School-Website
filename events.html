Events

At Timphisini High we ocasionally host events from time to time sometimes to celebrate our culture, to award excellence, to have fun, or most importantly worship God our saviour and ask for guidance everytime we start a new term. All these events are hosted locally in our luxurious school hall.

First up is Our prayer Day

Every start of term at Timphisini we have our Prayer Day to ask for protection from God as we start the term.Guest pastors are invited for a sermon and all the the students are assembled in the the school hall where it is held, our S.U team members are the ones who organise this glorious day below is a short clip of our latest prayer day.

Which brings us to our SU programme

This a programme which is lead by some of our teachers it is all about glorifying God at all times.Here at Timphisini we are a Christian school so at 10min before the bell for the lessons rings SU members assembly at a class for a short word of God and everyone is welcome to join.

Our Speech and Price Giving Day


At Timphisini we believe in Excellence and hard work also that hardwork really does pay off. So during this day all those students who dared to be excellent and rise above the rest are awarded with gifts and certificates signed by our beutiful Headteacher, guest speakers are also invited together with parents and some former students. Speeches are also given out by our guest speakers an sometimes by our admin, the main aim of this day is to award excellence and promote hardwork to achieve excellence
